Affordable

Legal bills should never surprise, outrage, or confuse a client. Two Rivers Law offers a range of affordable and easy-to-understand billing arrangements, including flat-fees, hourly billing capped at fixed amounts, and sliding-scale rates based on a client's ability to pay. We do not mark up costs and expenses. We make heavy use of the internet and virtual office solutions to keep our overhead low. We offer a free, 15-minute phone consultation to determine whether we can help with your matter. If we are not a good match, you will receive appropriate referrals.



Only What You Need

Some clients want a lot of attorney involvement, while others seek only limited assistance. Many simply want an overview of an area of law relevant to their activities or a working understanding of a business document. If you only need minor tweaks to an existing document or a brief push in the right direction, we are happy to do precisely that. We unbundle our services to provide only those that you need and want.




Committed to Professionalism

Attorneys should be educators, advocates, and peacemakers. For us, professional service means excellent and timely communication with clients as well as respectful and prompt interaction with other parties. Two Rivers Law complies with the Oregon State Bar's Statement of Professionalism and the Washington State Bar's Creed of Professionalism.
